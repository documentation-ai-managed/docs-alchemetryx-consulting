## Overview
The dashboard centralizes key business data for decision-making.

## Setup Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into your account">
    ````
    Go to `https://dashboard.alchemetryx.com` and enter your credentials.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect source apps">
    ````
    From the Integrations tab, link tools like QuickBooks, CRMs, or Google Sheets.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize widgets">
    ````
    Select business metrics (finances, follow-ups, projects) you want to track.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
Need help? Email support@alchemetryx.com for guided setup.
</Callout>
